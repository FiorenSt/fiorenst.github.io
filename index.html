About Me

Who Am I?

My name is Fiorenzo Stoppa and I am an astrostatistician, which means that I use statistical methods to analyze data from astronomy and astrophysics.

I have always been fascinated by the mysteries of the universe and how we can learn about them through observation and experimentation. In my work, I use a wide range of techniques to extract meaningful insights from large and complex datasets, and to test hypotheses about the nature and evolution of celestial objects and phenomena.

I received my bachelor's and master's degrees in statistics from the University of Bologna, and have been working in the field of astrostatistics for the past three years.

On this website, you can find out more about my research interests, my publications, and my professional experience. You can also read about some of the topics that I find particularly interesting and thought-provoking, and get in touch with me if you have any questions or would like to discuss any of these topics further.

I hope you enjoy your visit, and thank you for stopping by!
